The be_founders-employees database

This database contains rich micro-data about the employees (n=99,432) from a sample Belgian start-ups (m=910). It is a spin-off from the be_founders project. Whereas be_founders contains data about start-up founders only, be_founders_employees contains information about the founders and the employees.

Using the be_founders start-ups as a starting point, we developed bots to find all their employees in a well-known employment-focused social media platform, and to download publicly-available data about their employment and education history. We developed NLP tools to pre-process, parse and disambiguate the data, and the resulting database includes among other things:

  • The individuals that were employed by the start-ups (up until 2022)

  • Their work experience (employer, role, etc.)

  • Their educational background (university/school, diploma, etc.)

  • Basic demographic data

The data allows to approximate the personnel base of each start-up over time, with rich information about turnover and churn, but employee skills, experience, and networks.
