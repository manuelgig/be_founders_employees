4.3. Schema: public

4.3.1. employee_turnover

Table 4.30 employee_turnover

column_name

data_type

description

organization_id

text

Belgian start-up unique id

year

bigint

roles_start

bigint

Number of new roles that started that year. Note that role start <> new hires. A new hire indicates a person that did not work in the company before has joine the company, whereas a role start indicates that a person (new or not new in the organization has taken a new role)

roles_end

bigint

Number of roles that ended that year. We consider that a role ended when a person finished a working spell, either because she left the company, or because she moved to a different role. The role is considered ended even if it was taken up by a different person.

hires

bigint

Number of employees who joined the firm in the focal year (i.e., new employees)

exits

bigint

Number of employees that left the firm in the focal year

hires_internal

bigint

Number of internal hires

workforce

bigint

Number of employees at the end of the focal year, calculated as the cumulative sum of (external) hires minus employee exits
